TRYBE05 / 12 · Diagnosis

The review wasn't broken in execution.
It was broken in design.

01 · Anchor

Anchored to the JD, not to impact.

Performance scored against role descriptions written at hire. People rated themselves "doing the job" — which they were. Commercial outcomes never entered the conversation.

02 · Cadence

Twice a year. Then forgotten.

Six-month gaps between signal and consequence. Managers couldn't remember February by August. Ratings drifted toward the mean by default.

03 · Distribution

No calibration. No real curve.

Every manager rated their own. Without cross-team calibration, ratings inflated and comp drifted off signal. Talent density was invisible.

TRYBE06 / 12 · Rebuild
Impact Factor v2.0

From politeness machine to signal engine.

"Score the outcome, not the activity. Then calibrate it together, in the open, on a power curve — and let comp follow signal."
Anchor

Role-specific commercial metrics replace JD-adherence as the scoring spine.

Curve

Power curve, not bell curve. Most cluster mid; outliers earn outsized recognition.

Calibration

Quarterly, cross-team, with structured evidence captured per rating.

Comp

Generated from rating × Impact Factor × tenure-adjusted band — not by tenure alone.

Growth

Enrichment (deepen craft) vs Enlargement (add scope) split into two different conversations.

TRYBE07 / 12 · The toolkit

Four frameworks that hold
the whole system together.

01
Impact Factor

Score impact, not activity.

Role-specific commercial metrics replace JD-adherence as the anchor.

02
TDX · Talent Density Index

Density over headcount.

A rolling single number for what % of the org is genuinely top-tier in role.

03
AMO Engagement

Signal that points somewhere.

Survey against Ability, Motivation, Opportunity — the three levers a manager can actually move.

04
Enrichment vs Enlargement

Two growth conversations.

Enrichment deepens craft. Enlargement adds scope. Mixing them promotes people into the wrong job.

TRYBE11 / 12 · First 90 days
First 90 days

Listen.
Map.
Ship.

No grand redesign in week one. Baseline first, codify second, ship one calibration moment in the open by day 90.
Days 0 – 30 · Listen

Baseline the truth.

  • 1:1 with every Trybe leader; 1:1 with five customers (spa ops directors).
  • Audit current ratings, comp bands, hiring funnel, engagement signal.
  • Map the org against TDX — what % is genuinely top-tier in role today.
  • Identify the two functions where calibration matters most this year.
Days 31 – 60 · Map

Define the operating system.

  • Draft Impact Factors per function, validated with leaders.
  • Set Trybe's first TDX target with the exec team.
  • Stand up the AMO survey instrument; quarterly cadence agreed.
  • Shortlist three AI tools to build first (sourcing, transcript, comp).
Days 61 – 90 · Ship

One calibration moment, in the open.

  • Run the first cross-function calibration session with exec.
  • Publish the comp philosophy and pay-band map internally.
  • Ship the sourcing agent in production for two open roles.
  • End-of-quarter: present TDX baseline + 12-month plan to the board.
